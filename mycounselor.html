About Me

My success is based on your feeling better at the end of our session than at the beginning and by your progress in addressing the challenges you face from one session to the next.
I have been a counselor and psychotherapy practitioner since 2007 and have met with a wide spectrum of clients in all age groups, from teenagers to seniors. My approach is to elicit and reinforce your positive attributes and competencies and to help you access your inner strength. I will never put myself above you. Even more than I hope to be a teacher to you, I am confident that you will be a teacher to me. I look forward to working together and accompanying you on the exciting journey towards the success, fulfillment, and happiness that you deserve.
